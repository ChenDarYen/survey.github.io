Image Preference Survey

Instructions

You will be shown a series of image pairs along with a text prompt.

  1. Read the prompt description carefully.
  2. Compare the two AI-generated images shown.
  3. Select the image that you personally prefer based on your overall judgment.
  4. Click the "I Prefer This Image" button under your preferred image.
  5. Repeat for all 20 prompts in the survey.

Important Notes

  • All images in this survey are generated by AI models
  • Images may not 100% match the prompts or may contain some distortions and imperfections

Evaluation Criteria

When making your choice, you may consider multiple aspects:

  • Image Quality: Overall clarity, presence/absence of distortions or artifacts
  • Prompt Alignment: How well the image captures the prompt's description
  • Visual Appeal: General attractiveness and artistic quality
  • Composition: Layout, colors, and overall design

Remember

  • There are no right or wrong answers
  • Trust your personal judgment and aesthetic preferences
  • Focus on which image you find more successful overall

Your thoughtful responses will help us understand and improve AI image generation technology. Thank you for participating!

Note: By clicking the "Start Survey" button, you will be redirected to the survey page.
